# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Contributors must communicate respectfully, accept technical feedback in good faith, and keep discussions focused on the project. Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, and publication of another person's private information are not accepted.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact methods on the maintainers' GitHub profiles. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially as practical.
